Chambers' Background

The law profession is an instrument of preventing lawlessness and disorderliness in any society. It serves the purpose of protecting and preserving the citizen’s rights as guaranteed by the Constitution.

Mack Ogbamosa & Co (Peace Chambers) law firm is committed to upholding and promoting the principles of rule of law and preservation of human rights. We believe in resolving conflicts among individuals, groups, and societies. We have been in practice for over a decade.

Our experience cuts across different areas of legal practice such as Litigation, Civil and Commercial law, Media law, Human Rights law, Property law, Financial laws and regulations, and General Corporate Practice. As Solicitors and Advocates, we continuously and vigorously pursue the ‘ideals of supremacy of the law, fair legal representation and accelerated hearing in all legal matters to ensure quick dispensation of justice.

We at Peace Chambers have in the course of our practice over the years consistently delivered quality legal services to our clients and shall continue to do so.

Firm's Profile

Established in Nigeria in 2009, Mack Ogbamosa & Co (Peace Chambers) is a law firm with offices in Lagos, Benin, Owerri and Uyo. We have a sizeable number of lawyers in our Chambers.

Our Principal Partner is Mr. Mack Ogbamosa. Other lawyers in the Chambers include: Okei Reuben Momah, Evans Ojilerere, Ebong Ekere, Samuel Abasilim and Fatai Abudu Umoru. We also have supporting staff consisting of Admin Manager, Secretary, Receptionist, Administrative Clerk and Head of Research Desk.

The firm has handled several civil and criminal cases from 2009 till date. We have also handled several legal issues bordering on contracts, perfection of legal documents and debt recoveries.

Our areas of specialization include Litigation, Civil and Commercial law, Financial laws and regulations, Oil and Gas law, Real Estate law, Labour law, Human Rights law and Media laws and regulations as well as General Corporate Services.
