About The Founder

Home Founder

Mack Ogbamosa is the Principal Partner of the law firm. He started his law practice in 2004 at Tony Momoh & Co. While there, he headed the sections on media laws, Labour/industrial law and Intellectual property law. He was also in charge of media law training and corporate services. He co- established the chambers in 2009.

  Before practising law, Ogbamosa worked as a journalist in the then largest circulating newspaper in the country- the DAILY TIMES which he joined automatically in 1984 as a Sub Editor after graduating from the then Times Journalism Institute,(formerly Times Newspaper Training Centre), Iganmu, Lagos.

While in the newspaper conglomerate, he rose to the positions of Chief Sub- Editor, Production Editor and Night (Alternate) Editor. He was at various times in the newsroom, Features Department and Editorial board. He won many prizes including those of Best Sub-Editor and Best Production Editor. He was also at various times, a member of the newspaper’s Editorial Handbook Team, Disciplinary Committee and Chairman, Nigeria Union Of Journalists (NUJ) Elections Committee.

He was a consultant to the Financial Reporting Council Of Nigeria(formerly Nigerian Accounting Standards Board) from 2005 to 2017. Ogbamosa holds a bachelor’s degree in Political Science, Second class (Upper Division), a professional diploma in Journalism and another Bachelor’s degree in Law. He is a solicitor and advocate of the Supreme Court of Nigeria.

  He belongs to many professional associations including the Nigerian Bar Association (NBA) and  Nigerian Institute of Public Relations (NIPR). He has written numerous articles for publication in several newspapers and journals.

They include: The Mass Media: Constitutional Responsibility Without Empowerment, Corporate Governance Challenges in Nigeria: The Way Forward, and Making Chapter 2 of The Nigerian Constitution Justiciable.
